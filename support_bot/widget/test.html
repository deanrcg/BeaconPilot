BeaconPilot Widget Test

About

This page demonstrates the BeaconPilot chat widget. The widget appears as a button in the bottom right corner. Click it to open the chat interface.

Installation

To add the widget to your website, simply add this script tag to your HTML:

<script src="http://localhost:8000/widget-js/widget.js"></script>

In production, replace localhost:8000 with your domain:

<script src="https://yourdomain.com/widget-js/widget.js"></script>
